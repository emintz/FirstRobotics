The ESP-32-S2 pinout is compatible with Espressif's 
ESP32-DevKitC development board whose schematic is
here: https://dl.espressif.com/dl/schematics/esp32_devkitc_v4-sch.pdf

When you read the schematic, note that  SENSOR_VP is GPIO36 and SENSOR_VN is GPIO39,
and that these are input-only. You can’t use them as output, and they do not have
a pullup or pulldown resistor!

The link and KiCad libraries for this board reside here: https://github.com/espressif/kicad-libraries
