## Background

The printed circuit board is a follow-on project to the prototype
countdown timer used to manage the practice field during the 2026
FIRST Mid-Atlantic championship competition. Everyone involved
considered it successful. The prototypes were immature, far too
fragile to withstand routine handling during transportation and
storage. It is hoped that the printed circuit board-based design
will meet this need.
